Logo Image Instructions:

1. Save your logo as 'logo.png' in this images folder
2. Recommended specifications:
   - Format: PNG (for transparency support)
   - Height: 80-120px (will be scaled to 40px)
   - Width: Proportional to height
   - Background: Transparent preferred
   - Style: Professional, clean design

3. Alternative formats supported:
   - logo.jpg
   - logo.svg (best for scalability)

4. If you don't have a logo yet:
   - The navigation will show a broken image icon
   - You can create a simple text-based logo
   - Or use online logo generators

Current setup:
- Logo height: 40px in navigation
- Max width: 150px
- Responsive and mobile-friendly
